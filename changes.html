Learn What's Inside Your Food! 🔬

🍞

Carbohydrates

What it does: Main energy source for your brain and body.

Food sources: Rice, Bread, Pasta, Fruits

Daily need: 6-11 servings

💪

Protein

What it does: Builds muscles, repairs tissues, supports growth!

Food sources: Eggs, Chicken, Fish, Beans

Daily need: 2-3 servings

🥦

Fiber

What it does: Aids digestion, keeps you full, prevents constipation!

Food sources: Vegetables, Oats, Whole grains

Daily need: 25-30 grams

🥩

Iron

What it does: Carries oxygen in blood, prevents tiredness!

Food sources: Spinach, Red meat, Beans

Daily need: 8-15 mg

🥛

Calcium

What it does: Builds strong bones and teeth!

Food sources: Milk, Cheese, Yogurt

Daily need: 1000-1300 mg

💧

Water

What it does: Keeps you hydrated, helps brain function!

Food sources: Water, Watermelon, Cucumber

Daily need: 8-10 glasses

☀️

Vitamin D

What it does: Helps absorb calcium for strong bones!

Food sources: Sunlight, Fortified milk, Eggs

Daily need: 600 IU

🍊

Vitamin C

What it does: Boosts immune system, helps healing!

Food sources: Citrus fruits, Bell peppers

Daily need: 65-90 mg

Allergy Replacement Guide 🚫

Have allergies? Find healthy alternatives for your favorite foods!

Egg Allergy

Can't eat eggs? Try these protein-rich replacements!

Replacements: Tofu, Beans, Chia Seeds, Flaxseed

Dairy (Milk) Allergy

Can't drink milk? Get calcium from these sources!

Replacements: Almond Milk, Soy Milk, Oat Milk, Leafy Greens

Gluten (Wheat) Allergy

Can't eat bread/pasta? Try these grains!

Replacements: Rice, Corn, Quinoa, Gluten-free Oats

Peanut Allergy

Can't eat peanut butter? Try these nut alternatives!

Replacements: Sunflower Butter, Soy Nuts, Pumpkin Seeds

Seafood Allergy

Can't eat fish? Get Omega-3 from these sources!

Replacements: Chicken, Beef, Walnuts, Flaxseeds

💡 Healthy Lifestyle Tips for Students

Healthy habits help you focus better in school and stay energized throughout the day. Try practicing these simple lifestyle tips!

🥤 Drink Water

Drink at least 8 glasses of water every day.

🍎 Eat Fruits Daily

Eat at least 2 fruits per day for vitamins.

🥦 Eat Vegetables

Fill half your plate with vegetables.

😴 Sleep Early

Sleep 8-10 hours every night.

📱 Limit Gadget Time

Avoid using gadgets 1 hour before sleep.

🏃 Exercise Daily

Move your body for at least 30 minutes.

🍳 Never Skip Breakfast

Breakfast helps your brain concentrate.

🍔 Reduce Junk Food

Limit fast food to once per week.

🧠 Relax Your Mind

Take short breaks during study.

👨‍👩‍👧 Eat With Family

Family meals encourage healthier eating.

🧼 Wash Hands

Wash hands before every meal.

🍚 Eat Balanced Meals

Include carbs, protein, and vegetables.

🌞 Get Sunlight

Sunlight gives your body Vitamin D.

🧍 Good Posture

Sit straight when studying or using gadgets.

😊 Stay Positive

A healthy mind helps your whole body.
